# Artifact 1 – Alder Staff

**Original Course:** GRA 202 – Introduction to 3D Modeling  
**Project:** Stylized Fantasy Staff  
**Capstone Artifact:** Artifact 1

---

## Purpose

This document records the original state of the artifact prior to beginning Capstone refinements. It establishes a baseline for measuring improvements throughout the project.

---

## Original Strengths

- Strong overall fantasy staff silhouette.
- Distinctive three-branch head design that creates a memorable profile.
- Clear visual separation between the staff head, shaft, and base.
- Stylized proportions suitable for a hand-crafted fantasy aesthetic.
- Solid foundation for further refinement and texturing.

---

## Areas Identified for Improvement

### Modeling

- Smooth the faceted appearance of the upper branch supports.
- Refine the transitions along the staff shaft to create more natural curves.
- Improve the silhouette while preserving the original design intent.

### Topology

- Reduce or eliminate unnecessary triangles and n-gons where practical.
- Improve edge flow to better support smoothing and UV unwrapping.

### UV Mapping

- Create a complete UV layout for the entire model.
- Organize UV islands efficiently to maximize texture space.

### Texturing

- Develop physically based materials using Adobe Substance 3D Painter.
- Replace the placeholder sphere with a stylized magical crystal.
- Create believable wood, crystal, and metal surface materials.
